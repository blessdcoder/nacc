Our Mission

Branded for Excellence through Christ Our Lord

The National Association of Catholic Corps members was established with the aim of nurturing the spiritual growth and development of Catholic Corps members during their service year. The Association has evolved over the years to encompass a wide range of activities and initiatives. It was founded on the core values of faith, selflessness, and service, with the ultimate goal of fostering unity among Corps members while promoting the teachings of the Catholic Faith across the nation...
